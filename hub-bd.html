What BD does

Business Development

Nothing in AIESEC happens in isolation — BD builds the external relationships every other hub depends on.

The team identifies and approaches potential partners, negotiates sponsorship and partnership agreements, and manages those relationships long after the contract is signed.

Key responsibilities

What the BD team owns

  • Identify and approach potential corporate and NGO partners
  • Negotiate sponsorship and partnership agreements
  • Maintain relationships with existing partners
  • Coordinate joint initiatives — CSR projects, hiring pipelines, hosting
  • Support the iCX hub with new host organisation leads
