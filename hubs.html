How we're organised

Seven hubs. One youth leadership movement.

Every exchange we deliver and every member we develop passes through one of seven functional hubs — each led by a Member Committee Vice President (MCVP) and powered by student volunteers.

01 · oGX

Outgoing Global Exchange

Sells and delivers Global Volunteer, Talent and Teacher experiences to Bangladeshi youth heading abroad.

Visit oGX
02 · iCX

Incoming Corporate Exchange

Sources international interns and volunteers and places them with companies and NGOs across Bangladesh.

Visit iCX
03 · MX

Member Experience

Recruits, trains, coaches and looks after the wellbeing of every member's leadership journey.

Visit MX
04 · EwA

Engage with AIESEC

Builds awareness on campuses and in communities, turning curiosity into our next applicants and members.

Visit EwA
05 · Finance

Finance

Manages budgeting, reporting, member dues and compliance to keep the entity financially sustainable.

Visit Finance
06 · MKT

Marketing

Owns the AIESEC Bangladesh brand — content, design, social media and digital growth.

Visit MKT
07 · BD

Business Development

Builds and grows partnerships with companies, NGOs and universities that power our programs.

Visit BD
