What oGX does

Outgoing Global Exchange

oGX is the team behind every Bangladeshi student who volunteers, interns or teaches abroad through AIESEC. From the first conversation on campus to the day they board their flight, oGX owns the experience.

The team promotes our exchange programs, guides applicants through selection, matches them to verified opportunities on AIESEC's global exchange platform, and runs the preparation seminars that get people ready to leave home.

Key responsibilities

What the oGX team owns

  • Promote & sell Global Volunteer, Talent and Teacher programs to students
  • Guide applicants from first enquiry through to acceptance
  • Match exchange participants to vetted opportunities abroad
  • Run mandatory pre-departure preparation seminars
  • Track the quality and satisfaction of every delivered experience
