Who we are

Activating leadership in every young person we reach

AIESEC in Bangladesh is a member of AIESEC International — a non-political, independent, not-for-profit organisation run entirely by young people, present in 120+ countries and territories.

Our story

A global movement, built locally

AIESEC was founded in 1948 by young people across seven countries who believed peace and human potential could be built through cross-cultural understanding. Today, that same idea drives over 40,000 leadership experiences delivered every year by youth, for youth.

AIESEC in Bangladesh carries that same mission into local universities and communities — equipping students with leadership experience long before they graduate, while connecting Bangladeshi talent to opportunities across the world.

Our purpose

Peace and the full development of human potential

Every project, exchange and meeting we run exists to develop leadership qualities in young people — so they go on to create positive change wherever they end up.

Mission

To engage and develop every young person in Bangladesh through leadership experiences that create positive impact on society.

Vision

Peace and fulfilment of humankind's potential — achieved one leadership experience, one exchange, one project at a time.

How we measure it

Through the number of quality leadership experiences delivered each year — exchanges, internships and member development combined.

The AIESEC Experience

Three phases, one leadership journey

Whether you join as a member or apply for an exchange, every AIESEC experience moves through the same three phases.

1

Engage with AIESEC

Young people discover AIESEC through campus outreach, events and our EwA hub.

2

Experiential Leadership Development

Members and exchange participants take on real responsibility — projects, exchanges, internships.

3

Life-Long Connection

Alumni stay connected as mentors, partners and advocates for youth leadership.
