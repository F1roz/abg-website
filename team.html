How we're structured

One President, seven hub leads, one shared goal

Each MCVP owns the strategy, budget and people of their hub, and sits with the MCP on the Member Committee — the entity's highest decision-making body for the term.

MCP — Member Committee President

Sets entity-wide strategy, represents AIESEC in Bangladesh externally, and aligns all seven hubs toward shared termly goals.
