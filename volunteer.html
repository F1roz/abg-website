Why volunteer

Real growth happens outside your comfort zone

Reading about global issues is one thing. Standing in front of a classroom in a country you've never visited, with people who don't speak your language, is another. That's where real leadership gets built.

  • Work directly on a cause you care about — education, environment or equality.
  • Live with a host community and build a genuinely global network.
  • Return with leadership, communication and adaptability employers notice.
How it works

A process built on 75+ years of sending youth abroad

1

Project coordination

We partner with vetted NGOs, schools and social enterprises abroad to design projects with real impact.

2

Logistics support

Guidance on visas, flights, airport pickup, accommodation and the basics you need on arrival.

3

Personal support

A dedicated AIESEC Bangladesh consultant plus a local AIESEC contact in your host country.

4

Pre & post-exchange seminars

Mandatory preparation sessions so you arrive ready, plus a debrief to turn the trip into a story you can tell.

Application process

From application to departure

1

Apply online

Tell us about yourself — an AIESEC representative will reach out for a short chat about your goals.

2

Find a match

Interview with the project manager of the opportunity you're interested in, with local support to prepare.

3

Sort logistics

Once accepted, we walk through the agreement and payment details together.

4

Prepare & depart

Visa and insurance support, plus workshops that get you ready to land running.

Fee structure

Applying is completely free. Program fees vary by project and destination, and many projects include food, accommodation or local transport — your AIESEC consultant will confirm exact costs for your chosen opportunity.
