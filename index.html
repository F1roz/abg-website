How we're organised

One organisation, seven functional hubs

Every part of AIESEC in Bangladesh — from sending students abroad to managing our finances — runs through one of seven functional hubs, each led by a member of our MC team.

oGX

Outgoing Exchange

Sends Bangladeshi youth abroad on volunteer, internship and teaching experiences.

Learn more
iCX

Incoming Exchange

Brings international interns and volunteers into Bangladeshi host companies and projects.

MX

Member Experience

Recruits, trains and develops every member's leadership journey inside AIESEC.
