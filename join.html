Lead a real team before you ever lead a real job

Join AIESEC in Bangladesh and step into one of seven functional hubs — running real projects, managing real budgets and people, from your very first semester.

Why join

What membership actually gives you

Not a certificate. Not a line on a CV. A genuine, practical leadership track.

Management experience

Run real projects and lead real teammates, with accountability for real outcomes.

Global network

Connect with members and alumni in 2,000+ universities across 120+ countries.

Career readiness

Build problem-solving, business insight and people skills employers consistently ask for.

A voice that's heard

From your first term, your ideas shape real projects — not just committee notes.

2
